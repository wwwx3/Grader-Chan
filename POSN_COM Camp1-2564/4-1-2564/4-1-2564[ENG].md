## Mother's Day (10 points)

The Thai calendar has the following month order and number of days, and the following weekday order.

### Months and Days

| No. | Month      | Days |
|-----|------------|------|
| 1  | January   | 31 |
| 2  | February  | 28 |
| 3  | March     | 31 |
| 4  | April     | 30 |
| 5  | May       | 31 |
| 6  | June      | 30 |
| 7  | July      | 31 |
| 8  | August    | 31 |
| 9  | September | 30 |
| 10 | October   | 31 |
| 11 | November  | 30 |
| 12 | December  | 31 |

### Weekday Order

| No. | Day |
|-----|-----|
| 1 | Sunday |
| 2 | Monday |
| 3 | Tuesday |
| 4 | Wednesday |
| 5 | Thursday |
| 6 | Friday |
| 7 | Saturday |

Thailand’s National Mother’s Day is on August 12 every year.

Write a program that reads two integers **m** and **d**, where  
- **m** is the month number  
- **d** is the weekday number of the 1st day of month **m**  

Output one integer: the weekday number of Mother’s Day.

---

### Input  
- Line 1: Integer **m**, the month number  
- Line 2: Integer **d**, the weekday number of the 1st day of month **m**

---

### Output  
- One line displaying the weekday number of Mother’s Day

---

### Example  

**Input**
```

2
3

```

**Output**
```

6

```

This means: if the input month is February and the 1st day of that month is Tuesday, then Mother’s Day will be Friday.
